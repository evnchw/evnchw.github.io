Academic-industry research partnerships (2022-Present)

Under ADIA Lab, the scientific research institution of ADIA, I have supervised a number of short-term research projects in data science for various university programs and departments, usually MSc-level capstones. Below is a running list.
  • LSE (2024-Present), PhD Economics
    • Mean field congestion in dry bulk shipping.
  • LSE (2023-Present), MSc Data Science
    • Mean field games in global shipping flows.
  • UCL (2024-Present), MSc Computer Science
    • Detecting structural breaks in spatial agricultural grids.
  • UCL/Oxford (2023-24), MSc MCF
    • Nowcasting global trade with time series signatures & shipping data.
  • U. of Toronto (2024), MFRM (Master of Financial Risk Management)
    • Statistics of UN Comtrade.
  • U. of Toronto (2023), MFRM
    • Nowcasting inflation with the Baltic Indices.
  • Institut Polytechnique de Paris / Ecole Polytechnique (2023-24), M2DS
    • The global shipping graph.
  • Ecole des Ponts ParisTech (2023), M2
    • The economic world through the lens of shipping.
  • Khalifa University (2022), MSc Computational Data Science
    • Analysis of corporate supply chains.
  • Khalifa University (2022), MSc Comp. DS
    • Studies in institutional ownership & geographical revenue.
